## 1. Platform Access and Multi-Level Security
Velofolio employs professional grade security to ensure the absolute integrity of your business data.

### 1.1. Registration and Identity
- **Role Selection**: New users select between SuperAdmin, Admin, and Editor roles during registration.
- **Credential Establishment**: Professional email validation and encrypted password requirements.

### 1.2. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- **Status Oversight**: View current 2FA status (Enabled/Disabled) within security settings.
- **Recovery Logic**: Access to professional account recovery forms to prevent lockout.
- **Active Sessions**: A dedicated card showing every device currently logged into your account, with the ability to terminate suspicious sessions.

## 2. Interface Architecture and Navigation
The design is optimized for a fast, responsive, and intuitive user journey.

### 2.1. Dynamic Global Navigation
- **Workspace Switching**: Instantly toggle between different business entities or departments.
- **Active State Highlighting**: The navigation bar professionally highlights your current location.
- **Enhanced Sidebar**: Direct access to Dashboard, Leads, Clients, Jobs, Production, Invoices, Calendar, Payments, Team, and Reports.

### 2.2. Interactive Search and Actions
- **Global Search**: A powerful search bar that indexes clients, lead names, emails, and job titles in real time.
- **Add Button Architecture**: Context-aware floating action buttons for creating leads, clients, or jobs from any screen.

## 3. Dashboard: Real-Time Business Management

The Dashboard provides a refined, non-technical view of your studio's operational health.

### 3.1. Compact Analytics Visuals
- **Interactive Graphing**: A streamlined line chart allowing users to toggle specific datasets such as Leads, Shoots (Production), and Revenue.
- **Time Range Precision**: One-click toggles for 7 Days, 30 Days, Month-to-Date (MTD), and Year-to-Date (YTD).
- **Custom Calendar Selection**: An integrated date picker for analyzing unique historical or seasonal performance.

## 4. Master Client and Company Registry
Transitioning from curiosity to a confirmed partnership happens within the Client module.

### 4.1. The Onboarding Process
- **Client vs. Company Tabs**: Instant switching during creation to capture either individual data or corporate organizational data.
- **Field Specifics**: Captures Company Name, Website, Primary Contact, Street Address, City, State, Postcode, Country, and Timezone.

### 4.2. Sorting and Data Management
- **Intelligent Sorting**: Organize your directory by Date Created, First Name, Last Name, Email, or Phone.
- **Data Mobility**: Professional tools to Import Client lists (CSV) or Export your database for external reporting.

## 5. Deep Dive: The Client Profile Experience
The Client Profile is the primary interface for individual customer oversight.


### 5.1. Overview Features
- **Visual Team Avatars**: See exactly which staff members are assigned to a client via their profile photos.
- **Lead Source Logic**: Identifies where the client came from (e.g., Instagram, Referral) with professional iconography.
- **Internal Notes Editor**: A full-width space for record keeping, such as "Client prefers candid shots" or "Requested pastel color palette."

### 5.2. Event Detail Cards
- **Status Indicators**: Color coded status tags (COMPLETED in Green, IN PROGRESS in Blue, NOT STARTED in Red).
- **Staff Attendance**: List of specific team members assigned to each individual event.

## 6. Integrated Contract and Document Management
Manage high-value legal and identity documents directly within the customer profile.

### 6.1. The Document Repository
- **Standard Document Types**: Manage specialized files including Federal Tax documents, Driving Licenses, Social Security records, Resale certificates, and Voided Checks.
- **Status Lifecycle**: Documents are tracked as Draft, Pending, or Signed.
- **Audit Data**: Each file records the specific Email and Role (Dealer, Customer, Admin, Vendor) of the person who uploaded the record.

## 11. System-wide Automation and Preferences
Configure the professional "autopilot" settings for your studio operations.

### 11.1. File Automation Rules
- **Client Access**: Toggle switch to Allow or Prevent Clients from uploading files directly into their portals.
- **Automated Billing**: Optional toggle for "Auto Send Invoice Email" upon document creation.
- **Debt Recovery**: Toggle "Auto Remind Overdue" to automatically follow up on unpaid balances.

### 11.2. Operational Thresholds
- **Reminder Intervals**: Configurable settings to send follow-up reminders after 3 Days, 5 Days, or 7 Days.
- **Data Thresholds**: Set Global Max File Upload limits (10MB, 30MB, or 50MB) to manage server storage and professional delivery speeds.
